About Us

Mission

Awareness: Increase awareness about MIS and OSCM majors through organizing information sessions, hosting guest speakers, and providing orientation programs about the majors, as well as additional information about professional certifications and careers.

Skills development: Provide training programs and seminars to develop technical skills (IT skills, analytics, quantitative) and professional skills (formal writing, presentation skills, etc.) of CBA students that are within the scope of MIS and OSCM careers.

Creating collegial environment: Promote a positive academic environment that motivates student participation in extracurricular activities, encourage collaboration, alleviate student stress and enforce high ethical values.
